Our Mission

Our mission is to increase the amount of students who want to pursue STEM careers. We will accomplish this by imersing kids into fun games and add a little bit of STEM learning in there in order for them to have fun and learn that STEM isn't boring. Understanding how to think like an engineer can help children problem solve real world issues at a young age.

Points

Students can earn points from finishing each game which can be used to help support issues around the world like deforestation, pollution in the oceans, and feeding America.
